How to read the index

WTI aggregates structured country, event, and group signals into a comparative monitoring index. Scores indicate relative analytical conditions at the dataset timestamp; they are not predictions or operational advice.

Review freshness, coverage, country evidence, and source context together. SDCofA maintains the analytical model and publishes the generated WTI dataset for this interface.
